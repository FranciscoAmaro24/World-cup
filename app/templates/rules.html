How to Play

Three ways to earn points
01
MATCH PREDICTIONS
Predict the score of every game

Before each match kicks off, submit your predicted score. The closer you are, the more points you earn.

  • Exact score e.g. you predict 2-1, it ends 2-1
  • Correct result Right winner or draw, wrong score
  • Wrong Incorrect result = 0 points
Predictions lock the moment the referee blows the opening whistle. Submit early!
Points per league (configurable by admin)
Exact score 3 pts
Correct result 1 pt
Wrong result 0 pts
Boost (exact score x2) 6 pts

Default values shown. Each league can set custom points.

02
BRACKET PICKS
Predict the knockout stage before it starts

Before the first knockout match, pick which teams you think will go deep. The further your picks advance, the more you score.

PICK YOU PREDICT...
4 teams reach the semi-finals
2 teams reach the final
1 team finishes as runner-up
1 team wins the World Cup
Bracket picks lock when the first R16 match kicks off. You cannot change them after.
Points per correct pick
Quarter-finalist (top 8) 1 pt
Semi-finalist (top 4) 2 pts
Finalist (runner-up) 5 pts
Tournament winner 10 pts

Default values shown. Each league can set custom points.

03
SWEEPSTAKE
Each player draws a team. Their tournament is your points.

Every paid member is randomly assigned one or more teams from the draw. The more your team wins, scores and keeps clean sheets, the more sweepstake points you earn.

How the draw works
  1. League admin enables sweepstake and sets the buy-in
  2. Members pay and are marked as paid by the admin
  3. Admin creates draw groups (e.g. Top Seeds, Mid, Underdogs) and assigns teams to each
  4. Admin clicks Draw, each member gets 1 random team per group
  5. Points accumulate as your teams win, score goals and keep clean sheets
  6. Each group can have its own win points (e.g. underdogs worth more per win)
If no groups are set up, teams are drawn randomly from all 48 squads.
